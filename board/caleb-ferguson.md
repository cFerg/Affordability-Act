  <section class="profile-section">
    <h2>About</h2>
    <p>
      With a background in software engineering and systems-oriented problem solving,
      Caleb created Affordability Act as a way to turn complex policy ideas into
      accessible, structured proposals that the public can evaluate, discuss, and refine.
    </p>
    <p>
      The project began as an independent effort to build a more transparent and
      actionable framework for affordability-focused reform, especially in areas
      such as housing, taxation, and cost of living.
    </p>
  </section>
