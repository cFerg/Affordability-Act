# Support Affordability Act

Affordability Act is currently an independent policy initiative focused on developing practical proposals to address cost-of-living challenges.

The project is in the process of forming a nonprofit organization to support long-term policy development and public engagement.

</header>

<section class="donate-section">

## Current Status

Affordability Act is **not yet registered as a nonprofit organization**.

This means that **donations are not currently tax-deductible**.

Formal nonprofit registration is currently being prepared, including the creation of bylaws and the formation of a founding board of directors.

Once the nonprofit is finalized, donations will become eligible for tax documentation where applicable.

</section>

<section class="donate-section">

## Early Support

If you would still like to help support the development of the project, small voluntary contributions can help cover:

- website hosting and infrastructure
- outreach materials
- policy research time
- platform development
- administrative setup costs

Early contributions help move the project toward becoming a fully established nonprofit organization.

</section>

<section class="donate-section donate-warning">

### A note about larger donations

Because nonprofit registration is still in progress, **large contributions are not recommended at this time** if you are seeking a tax deduction.

Once the nonprofit structure is finalized, this page will be updated with official donation options and tax documentation details.

</section>
