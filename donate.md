<h1>Support Affordability Act</h1>

<p>
Affordability Act is currently an independent policy initiative focused on developing practical proposals to address cost-of-living challenges.
</p>

<p>
The project is in the process of forming a nonprofit organization to support long-term policy development and public engagement.
</p>

<section class="donate-section">

<h2>Current Status</h2>

<p>
Affordability Act is <strong>not yet registered as a nonprofit organization</strong>.
</p>

<p>
Because of this, donations made at this time are <strong>not tax-deductible</strong>.
</p>

<p>
The organization is currently preparing bylaws and forming a founding board of directors as part of the nonprofit registration process.
</p>

</section>
